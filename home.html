About the Instructor

"Hello! I'm Katelyn Pfundstein, a passionate and dedicated music educator with a degree in Music Education from Olivet Nazarene University. With four enriching years of experience as a music teacher, I have had the privilege of nurturing the musical talents of countless students. Through innovative teaching methods and a deep understanding of diverse musical genres, I strive to create a dynamic and engaging learning environment that empowers students to explore their creativity and unlock their full potential. Join me in the joy of music education, where we can harmonize our passion for music and cultivate a lifelong appreciation for the art form together!"
